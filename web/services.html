We specialize in the following areas

Rexbrize Oil Limited is incorporated in Nigeria as a private indigenous company to provide professional Engineering services in the fields of Electrical/Mechanical Instrumentation, Calibration, Pressure-Testing/Hydro-Testing, PD Metering/prover-loop, Inspection/NDT services.

Non-Destructive Test

Rexbrize Oil & Gas limits offers a complete range of non-destructive testing (NDT), visual inspection and materials sampling inspection capabilities. Our inspection teams are qualified to international standards and are supported by the expertise of our inspection department and associated affiliates, backed with the latest technology and tools in the industry.

Calibration and Leak Test Services

Our bespoke design and testing methodologies have assisted our clients in their pre-commissioning plans through the hydrostatic or pneumatic testing process. This is carried out by the industry’s statutory requirements. And our ISO/IEC 17025 laboratory gives us the leeway to have a precise equipment to all our stakeholders.

Lifting Equipment Inspection

Rexbrize provides a comprehensive range of inspection and NDT services for lifting gears across all industry sectors. We conduct thorough examinations every 6months, for lifting equipment and any associated accessories used to lift people. We conduct inspections to ensure every component of our machine is sound and fully operational.

Gas Monitoring Equipment

We are the sole agents for Crowcon Gas Detection Instruments in Nigeria, offering sales, calibration and maintenance services. We also calibrate and maintain several other brands of Gas Detectors such as MSA, Dragger, RKI, BW Technology, Honeywell. The gas sensors continuously detect the presence of gas and send the data to the control unit, which displays the gas concentration and triggers the alarms. Its designed to measure the presence & concentration of gases.

Project Commissioning

We provide project commissioning services - design, installation, testing, operation, handover and maintenance. Our team of project engineers ensures that the tenets and attributes of project management from project initiation/planning to closing is implemented in the life cycle of your projects to meet expected targets.

Wellhead Services

We provide cost effective wellhead services. Our experience and expertise ensures that we can install and maintain all types of wellheads. Our maintenance activities are supported by detailed procedures that reflect API specifications, and risk assessments that take account of recognized safety regulations. We offer: » Wellhead Installation Services » Wellhead maintenance » Pressure bleeding / monitoring » Visual inspections » Function testing of valves, chokes and actuators » Pressure testing of valves and interfaces » Inflow testing » Maintenance databases » Non-destructive testing » Lubrication » Meter Proofing » Provision of Wellhead Equipments & accessories » Valve fitting
